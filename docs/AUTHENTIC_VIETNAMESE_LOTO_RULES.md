# Authentic Vietnamese Lô Tô Game Rules

## Research Summary

Based on extensive research of traditional Vietnamese Lô Tô game rules, here are the **authentic specifications**:

---

## 1. Game Components

### Traditional Set Composition
- **90 numbered pieces** (cờ lô tô): numbered from **1 to 90**
- **16 paper cards/tickets** (tấm vé)
- **1 cloth bag** to hold the numbered pieces
- Pieces made of wood or plastic

**Source:** [Wikipedia Lô tô](https://vi.wikipedia.org/wiki/L%C3%B4_t%C3%B4), [Bách Hóa Xanh Guide](https://www.bachhoaxanh.com/kinh-nghiem-hay/huong-dan-cach-choi-lo-to-giay-chi-tiet-tuyen-tap-cau-rao-hay-nhat-1589467)

## 2. Card/Ticket Format

### Traditional Vietnamese Lô Tô Card Layout

**Card Structure:**
- Each card has **3 rows** × **9 columns** = 27 cells
- **15 numbers per card** (exactly)
- **12 blank cells** per card
- Each row contains **5 numbers** and **4 blanks**
- Numbers range from **1-90** (NOT 0-99)

**Column Distribution (Critical Rule):**
- Column 1: Numbers 1-9
- Column 2: Numbers 10-19
- Column 3: Numbers 20-29
- Column 4: Numbers 30-39
- Column 5: Numbers 40-49
- Column 6: Numbers 50-59
- Column 7: Numbers 60-69
- Column 8: Numbers 70-79
- Column 9: Numbers 80-90

**Example Card Layout:**

├────┼────┼────┼────┼────┼────┼────┼────┼────┤

├────┼────┼────┼────┼────┼────┼────┼────┼────┤

## 3. Win Conditions

### Traditional Win Rule: **ONE ROW = WIN**

- **Primary Win:** Complete **1 horizontal row** (all 5 numbers marked)
- When a player completes a row, they shout **"Kinh!"** (or "Lô tô!")
- The caller verifies the numbers match the drawn numbers
- First person to complete a row wins that round

**Important Notes:**
- Only **horizontal rows** count (not columns, diagonals, or full card)
- **One row is sufficient to win**
- Multiple players can win in same round if rows completed simultaneously

**Source:** [Luật Minh Khuê](https://luatminhkhue.vn/choi-lo-to-la-gi.aspx), [PrintGo Guide](https://printgo.vn/cach-choi-lo-to-hieu-qua-v4468), [Shopee Blog](https://shopee.vn/blog/cach-choi-lo-to/)

## 4. Game Flow

### How to Play

1. **Setup:**
   - Gather players (typically 2-16 players)
   - Select one person as caller/"cái" (house)
   - Each player selects **one or more cards** to play

2. **Gameplay:**
   - Caller draws random numbered pieces (1-90) from bag
   - Caller announces each number (often with rhyming phrases)
   - Players mark numbers on their cards if they have them
   - Continue until someone completes a row

3. **Winning:**
   - Player with complete row shouts "Kinh!" or "Lô tô!"
   - Caller verifies the marked numbers
   - If correct, player wins that round
   - Game can continue for multiple rounds

**Source:** [Thư Viện Pháp Luật](https://thuvienphapluat.vn/cong-dong-dan-luat/huong-dan-cach-choi-lo-to-choi-lo-to-co-phai-danh-bac-khong-209218.html), [My Kingdom](https://www.mykingdom.com.vn/blogs/choi-cung-con/tro-choi-lo-to)

## 5. Multiple Cards

### Players Can Hold Multiple Cards

**Traditional Rule:**
- Each player can select **one or more cards** (tấm vé) to play
- More cards = higher chance of winning (but harder to track)
- Standard sets come with 16 cards for up to 16 players
- If fewer players, they can take multiple cards each

**Source:** [GifGo Guide](https://gifgo.vn/mach-ban-cach-choi-lo-to-chuan-de-hieu-va-chien-thang-de-dang-v4299), [Công Lý](https://congly.com.vn/cach-choi-lo-to/)

## 6. Cultural Context

### Traditional Vietnamese Family Game

- Played during **Tết (Lunar New Year)** celebrations
- Family gathering activity
- Simple entertainment with light betting (small amounts)
- Caller often uses **rhyming phrases** to announce numbers
  - Example: "Số 11 - hai cây giậu" (Number 11 - two sticks)
  - Example: "Số 69 - mười chín nụ hôn" (Number 69 - nineteen kisses)

**Source:** [Vietcetera Cultural Article](https://vietcetera.com/en/feeling-lucky-try-this-vietnamese-traditional-game-called-lo-to), [AN Tours Vietnam](https://antoursvietnam.com/what-is-lotto-show-lo-to/)
